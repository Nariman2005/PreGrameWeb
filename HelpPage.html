Common Issues

How do I create an account?
To create an account, click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner. You'll need to provide a valid email address, create a password, and select your user type (Gamer, Tester, or Developer). After registration, verify your email to activate your account.
I forgot my password. How can I reset it?
Click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password.
How do I verify my account?
After registration, check your email for a verification link. Click the link to verify your account. If you didn't receive the email, check your spam folder or request a new verification email from your account settings.

For Gamers

How do I download and play games?
Browse our game library and click on any game you're interested in. Click the "Download" button to start the download process. Once downloaded, launch the game through our platform's launcher.
What are the system requirements?
System requirements vary by game. You can find the specific requirements on each game's page. Generally, you'll need a Windows 10/11 PC with at least 8GB RAM and a dedicated graphics card for most games.
How do I report a bug in a game?
While playing, press F1 to open the bug report menu. Fill in the details of the issue, including steps to reproduce and any error messages. You can also attach screenshots or video recordings of the bug.

For Testers

How do I become a game tester?
To become a tester, complete your profile and select "Tester" as your role. You'll need to pass a basic testing knowledge assessment. Once approved, you can apply for testing opportunities in the Testing Hub.
How do I submit bug reports?
Use our Bug Report Tool to document issues. Include detailed steps to reproduce, expected vs. actual behavior, and any relevant screenshots or logs. Submit through the Testing Dashboard for review.
What are the testing guidelines?
Testers must follow our Testing Guidelines, which include thorough documentation, clear communication, and adherence to testing schedules. Guidelines are available in the Testing Hub under "Resources".

For Developers

How do I upload my game?
Go to the Developer Dashboard and click "Upload New Game". Follow the upload wizard to provide game details, upload files, and set up distribution settings. Your game will be reviewed before publication.
What are the game submission requirements?
Games must meet our technical requirements, include necessary documentation, and pass security checks. Required files include the game executable, installation instructions, and system requirements.
How do I update my published game?
Access your game in the Developer Dashboard and select "Update Game". Upload the new version and provide release notes. Updates will be reviewed before being pushed to users.
